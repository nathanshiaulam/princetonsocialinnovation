Vision

We believe in the power of sustainable social impact. We see a world in which the pursuit of socially responsible careers is the norm.

Mission

We educate students on viable methods of creating social value by hosting events that spark campus-wide discussions on social innovation and impact.

CORE VALUES


INSPIRE

Host events for students that expand their knowledge on ways to integrate social impact into their lives.


CONNECT

Serve as a nexus for students to form relationships with individuals and groups that provide them with opportunities that create social impact.


EMPOWER

Encourage students to transform the knowledge and networks we provide into tangible impact.
